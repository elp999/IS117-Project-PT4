What foods are healthy

Some of the top common healthy foods include fruits like berries, apples, and bananas, which are rich in vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants. Leafy greens such as spinach, kale, and broccoli are packed with essential nutrients like calcium, iron, and vitamins A and C. Whole grains like quinoa, brown rice, and oats provide fiber, protein, and various vitamins and minerals. Lean proteins like chicken breast, fish, tofu, and beans offer essential amino acids and are low in unhealthy fats. Additionally, nuts and seeds like almonds, chia seeds, and flaxseeds are excellent sources of healthy fats, protein, and fiber. Incorporating these foods into your diet can contribute to overall health and well-being.
